Principles

Characteristics → Principles

  • Build for flexibility; the decisions you make now can limit your future.
  • Choose your tools wisely.
  • Document everything; so you can forget it.
  • Automate everything; so you won't have to do it.
  • Be suspicious of things that take too long. These quickly become roadblocks.
  • Make it configurable, within reason.
  • Be cautious adopting new technology. Code reuse isn't always good.
